7. Are there any does & don't I need to take care of while uploading my photograph?
  • i. The image file should be in JPG or GIF Format
  • ii. The size of each photograph must not exceed 100KB.
  • iii. Do note that the ideal width & height must be 3.5 X 5 inches (Postcard Size) in 72 dpi.
  • iv. Moreover, your chosen photograph must be in accordance with the socially acceptable standards. Zibonshathi reserves the right to remove/delete any photograph that violates socially accepted norms of decency and/or contains any form of nudity.
  • v. Also, do not post caricatures or copyrighted images

8. Give me some advice/ tips to make my photograph look better
Here are some time tested tricks that have done wonders for our users :
  • i. Choose a solo photograph having a close-up or a full view. Keep away from group Photograph where you are only a small part of the whole photo because it raises confusion for the viewer and also encourages comparisons which may not always work to your advantage
  • ii. Professionally clicked snaps are would make a great difference in comparison to the ones clicked by you at random. Professionals are well versed with the right angles, lightening and backdrops and adjust them to make you look your best. So getting a snap professionally clicked is definitely worth the effort.
  • iii. Choose photograph where you are the focus of attention and not the scenery surrounding you. Your prospective partner wants to judge you and not the beauty around you!!
  • iv. Consider picking up photograph that are bright and colorful. Photograph tend to become a little darker when seen on your desktop, so avoid posting Photograph shot in the night.
  • v. After selecting and scanning your photograph, do not forget to use Image Enhancing Softwares like Adobe PhotoShop or Corel Photopaint. Many good cyber cafes offer services in this regard. Ask someone trained there to adjust the brightness, hue, contrast and saturation levels to give you best results.
