SEARCHING AND MATCHING PROFILE
1. How can I search for my partner on Zibonshathi? What are the search options available?
The search on Zibonshathi has been designed in a user-friendly way so that it is always effective and yields the results that you are looking for. Essentially, 2 kinds of search options are available on Zibonshathi- Quick Search & Advanced Search.
2. What is the Quick Search & Advanced Search option?
The Quick Search option on Zibonshathi enables you to search fast using parameters like Gender, Age. The Advanced Search on the other hand, uses several more parameters like Gender, Age, Marital Status, Religion, Education, Occupation, Height, complexion . Both are effective searches and yield results efficiently. You are advised to fill in as many relevant parameters to get relevant results.
3. Adding profile to your Account:
After selecting a few profiles, when you click on this button, the selected profiles would be saved in the View Favorite list section. But remember, you should be logged in for using the service.
4. How do I search for my details :
You can search for your profile details. To search for your profile, just input your Email address and Password in the box with the caption Member Login and click on the button Login. Or click on Login link on the header and input your email address & password.
5. How do I change my password :
To do this click on Change password from My Account page. It will show you change password option.
6. How do I delete or edit my account :
After going My Account page you can edit / delete or modify your account as you want.
7. How do I read of write message:
From My account page you can read or write message to click on View Message link.
